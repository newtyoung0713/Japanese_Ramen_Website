The history of Ramen

    To begin with the history of ramen. There are many versions to talk about how the noodles became ramen from the ancient China to Japan; therefore, I picked some of them to share with.

  1. The first one was about six centuries ago. The original named Sutra Noodles about in Ming dynasty. However, in that moment, people rarely knew about it, until about two centuries ago that the noodles recipe was imported to Japan. Because of the earthquake in 1923, many areas got the problems, so people sold ramen by venders, and it was increased again after World War II. Day by day, about in 1958, a person sold the first instant ramen; moreover, by this event, ramen became a famous word from Japan to the whole world.
  2. Another story was around in 1910. There was a Chinese restaurant in Tokyo sold the ramen with shoyu based broth, sliced pork, narutomaki, vegetables and seaweeds. This soup noodles combination has become a standard of Tokyo ramen. Because of it was cheap, convenient, and delicious noodles, so it has been popular soon.
  3. The third story was kind of unconstrained. The story was based on the Japanese daimyo, Tokugawa Mitsukuni, that is a famous character in Japan. He had met with a homeless who was from the Ming dynasty, and one day that the homeless named Zhiyu Zhu made one noodle soup for Tokugawa Mitsukuni. Therefore, people said he was the first person had ramen in Japan, and the ramen was based on that noodle soup.

    4. There are different stories about the history of ramen in Japan has been from a vender to a popular food in the world; furthermore, it is a classic food for Japan.

And the style from the classic shio, shoyu, tonkotsu, and miso extend to some, such as, spicy miso, tonkotsu shoyu, and other different styles of spicy mala, tom yam kung, and pesto. However, in the past few years, many special flavors soup come up, like pineapple, chocolate, strawberry, and coffee and so on.

The pure series soup is making for some religion's people who cannot have the soup made by pork or who are vegetarian. Moreover, they have another type of ramen are for vegetarian, like tofu and vegetarian meat. About the broth, the most special one is tom yam kung that combine Thai style to Japanese ramen and with a steak.
    There are many different soups like I mentioned above. Also have different type of ramen, for instance, seafood (like grab, fishes, shellfish, lobster and so on), pork cartilage, tomato, spicy, cold noodles, chicken, and tsukemen. Not only soup based and ramen style but also the noodles style that each area has their own way, and every noodle has its own taste and shape. For example, Asahikawa ramen, Sapporo ramen, Kitakata ramen, and Tokyo ramen.

The etiquette of Ramen

    Furthermore, Japanese is one of ethnicity caring about etiquette. Not only in the normal life but also for eating ramen, they care about it as well. There are five points if you want to enjoy ramen in Japan.

  1. First, waiting for your turn, the reason is the seat less, so the customers must be patient for each seat.
  2. Second, eating quickly, because of ramen is a kind of fast food in Japan. Other reasons based one the first point and if eating slowly, the noodle gets soggy and limp easily.
  3. Third, one bowl for each person, this is also based on the first rule that limited seat.
  4. Fourth, order by ticket or staff, this situation depends on the ramen shop, and paid before or after.
  5. The last one, taste the soup first, enjoying the first sip for the original soup and do not have other toppings are the most respect way for the ramen shop in Japan; however, after the first sip of the soup, you can do whatever you like.

    6. I think there are more knowledge for the etiquette of ramen, but these five key points are the most way to show the etiquette.
